Aglaia Studio


A private journey into style where beauty becomes a dialogue between identity, aesthetics, and personal expression.

In ancient Greece, Aglaia, one of the Three Graces, embodied the brilliance of beauty — a radiance inseparable from form and deeply connected to adornment. Inspired by this idea and by a shared pursuit of harmony between the inner and outer self, Sarah and Agostino founded Aglaia Studio, a creative space devoted to a more personal and meaningful experience of style, art, and identity.

With backgrounds in fashion, art direction, and styling, their work draws from a mutual fascination with art, architecture, and design. Their vision moves beyond trends, shaping beauty as a balance between body and ornament, simplicity and detail, presence and expression.

Rather than traditional shopping, this experience becomes a curated exploration of taste — an intimate process designed around each client's personality, lifestyle, and aesthetic sensibility. Every session unfolds organically, combining professional styling expertise with carefully selected locations, independent boutiques, ateliers, and hidden addresses across Florence. Whether redefining a wardrobe, preparing for a special occasion, or discovering unique design pieces, the experience adapts fluidly to individual desires.



Private Shopping — Fashion & Design

Personal Styling — Fashion & Design

Event Styling & Grooming


Dedicated styling designed for a specific occasion, including outfit curation, shopping guidance, and full look coordination, with optional hair and makeup services.


Outlet & Boutique Tour


A curated route through selected boutiques and outlets to discover unique pieces and timeless finds, guided by expert aesthetic direction.
