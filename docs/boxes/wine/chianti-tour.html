Full Day Chianti Tour


A full-day journey into the heart of Chianti, visiting three family-run organic wineries off the beaten path — guided by Matteo, a local sommelier born and raised in Florence. Small group, authentic producers, and panoramic Tuscan landscapes throughout the day.

This is not a commercial wine tour. It's an intimate experience where wine, territory, and genuine human connection come together.
Each winery welcomes guests personally, sharing the stories behind their land, their grapes, and their craft.



Departure from Florence


The tour begins at 10:00 AM from a central meeting point in Florence. Guests travel together in a comfortable, air-conditioned 8-seat van through the rolling hills of Chianti toward the first estate.


Three Family-Owned Wineries


At each estate, guests are welcomed by the winemakers themselves for a guided visit through vineyards and cellars. The focus is on organic and sustainable farming, with wines that reflect the land, the season, and the people behind them.
At least four wines are tasted at each winery — twelve or more glasses in total.


Tuscan Lunch


A traditional lunch is served at one of the estates, accompanied by selected wine pairings and panoramic vineyard views. Vegetarian options are available on request.

  • Bruschette, olive oil tasting, salami and pecorino cheese
  • Two seasonal pasta dishes
  • Dessert and espresso

Return to Florence


The group returns to the Sant'Ambrogio area in central Florence at approximately 5:30 PM.
