Private Wine Experience in the Tuscan Countryside


An authentic Chianti Colli Fiorentini wine experience just outside Florence, hosted inside a historic villa dating back to the 15th century. Nestled among rolling hills and vineyards, Castelvecchio welcomes guests for an intimate and personal visit directly at the estate.

This is not a commercial wine tour, but a genuine eno-tourism experience where tradition, craftsmanship, and territory come together.
Guests are guided by the sommelier or the owner themselves, for a relaxed and personal conversation about wine, landscape, and Tuscan culture.



Welcome at the Estate


The visit begins with a warm welcome, followed by an introduction to the history of the Castelvecchio family and the origins of the winery. Weather permitting, a short walk through the vineyards can also be arranged upon request.


Cellar Visit


Guests will explore the historic cellar while learning about production and aging techniques and the characteristics of the Chianti Colli Fiorentini wine area.


Tasting


A welcome glass is served on the panoramic terrace overlooking the symbolic cypress tree of the estate, before moving to the tasting room.
The tasting includes:

  • Three top estate wines
  • Estate extra virgin olive oil with traditional Tuscan bread
  • Traditional Vin Santo paired with dark chocolate

Each tasting is conducted personally by the sommelier or the owner, encouraging an informal and engaging discussion about wine and territory.
