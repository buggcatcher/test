Pasta Fresca delle Cure — A True Local Institution

Just outside Florence's historic center, in the authentic neighborhood of Le Cure, Pasta Fresca delle Cure is a true local institution — part fresh pasta boutique, part neighborhood trattoria.

Here, handmade pasta is prepared daily following traditional recipes, attracting locals who stop by both to buy fresh pasta to cook at home and to enjoy a simple, honest lunch built around seasonal ingredients and classic Italian flavors.

It's not a tourist restaurant, but a real Florentine everyday spot — and absolutely worth the short trip outside the center.




  • Lunch only: Service is available only at lunch. Arrive early or be prepared for a short wait.
  • No reservations: If there's a queue, write your name in the notebook outside the entrance to secure your spot. Staff will call you when your table is ready.
  • Menu on the wall: No printed menus; daily options are displayed on the wall. Take a look as you enter to decide what to order.
  • Wine, local style: Served by the half-liter or liter at excellent prices.
  • Busy hours: During peak times, guests may be asked to leave their table after finishing, to accommodate waiting visitors.
  • Location tip: Le Cure is outside the tourist center, but visiting is worth it — authentic Florence, local life, and great food.
  • Planning tip: Ask Francesco which nearby experiences to combine with your visit for a perfect half-day.
